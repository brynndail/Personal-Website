While visiting my dad in Italy, we decided to hike what is called the Strade delle 52, or "52 Tunnels". This was definitely not the leisurely hike we were expecting (silly Americans!) and covered a little over 4 miles with an elevation change of 2,460ft. Though hard because we were ill-prepared, it was beautiful!
